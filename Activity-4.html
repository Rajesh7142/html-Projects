Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Minced meat - 300g
  • Tomato sauce - 1 cup
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add minced meat and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over spaghetti and enjoy.
